Learner's license

Eligibility Criteria:

1.)The minimum age required to obtain a learner’s licence for a Light Motor Vehicle in Karnataka is 18-years.

2.)The minimum age required to obtain a learner’s licence for a non-geared motorcycle up to 50cc engine capacity is 16-years. They should also possess a letter of consent from the parents or guardian.

3.)The minimum age required to obtain a learner’s licence for a Commercial Vehicle in Karnataka is 20-years.

4.)To obtain a learner’s licence for a commercial vehicle, the applicant must hold a permanent driving licence for a LMV.

5.)Anyone applying for a learner’s licence must have a knowledge of the Karnataka traffic rules and regulations.

Driving licence

procedure criteria:

1.)The form should be filled up with the correct details avoiding all the errors. The form number 4 should be downloaded by the applicant and then it should be submitted with the necessary documents.

2.)You can ask the RTO official authorities to schedule the driving test for you. Make sure that you reach at the location of the driving test on the given date and time. The driving test is taken by the concerned authorities at the RTO office.

3.)After passing the test, you will receive the driving license within thirty days by speed post.
