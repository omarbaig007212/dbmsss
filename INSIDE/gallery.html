About Us :

Organisation of the Indian government responsible for maintaining a database of drivers and a database of vehicles for Karnataka.

It issues driving licences, organises collection of vehicle excise duty and sells personalised registrations. It also is responsible to inspect vehicle's insurance and clear the pollution test.
